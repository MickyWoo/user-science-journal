Critical Role Checkout Link

Basis of the site is a ecommerce website. Im just using the checkout as reference for the assignment.Each

Criteria is listed below and the positive and negatives I may have found.



Criteria

  1. Scan Page
    • page is easily read from top to bottom and left to right, as in there arn't any unecessary words
    • Page is divided into two sections left half is information with white backround and right side is grey with total payment imformation
  2. No Clutter
    • bread crumbs to let us know the person is at checkout page OF store with logo showing.
    • no excess wording that may cause confusion when getting from point A > B
  3. Filling in the Blanks
    • each blank still holds name so people dont forget they are filling out the name information
    • states drop down option so people can just find their state by name instead of spelling out
  4. mindless clicking
    • "kart" icon is shown for easy checkout without second guessing
    • Main icons have css and slightly larger border like the "checkout" to be found easier
  5. purchase
    • images of item is shown to simplify item pruchased instead of reading
    • quantity is an icon top right corner
    • Total for bill is Larger font to be FOUND easier.
    • the One issue I may have is that the "amount" is numbered in the top right of the image of product shown instead of a "1x or 2x" for less confusion
